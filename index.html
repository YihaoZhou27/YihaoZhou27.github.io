Yihao Zhou

My name is Yihao Zhou. I am a fourth-year Astrophysics PhD student and FINESST Fellow at Carnegie Mellon University.

Research Interests

I use cosmological simulations to study the formation, evolution, and merger of massive black holes (MBHs). I try to provide interpretation/prediction for the detection of JWST, Pulsar Timing Arrays (PTA), and Laser Interferometer Space Antenna (LISA).

I developed simulations on different scales, from sub-parsec to Mpc, from the local universe to the early universe. Here are some of the simulations I have developed:

  • ASTRID
  • ASTRID-BRAHMA
  • MAGICS
